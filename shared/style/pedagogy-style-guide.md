# Pedagogy Style Guide

## General tone

- graduate-level but readable
- concept-first, then formalization
- explicit about assumptions
- clear about what is empirical versus theoretical

## Layering

Every module should visibly separate:
- Bridge
- Field Core
- Research Lab

## Repetition policy

Repeat only what improves learning.
For shared methods or definitions, link to the shared toolkit instead of rewriting the same note.

## Reading ladders

Every module should provide:
- 1 bridge reading
- 2 or 3 core readings
- 1 or 2 frontier readings

## Visuals

Prefer:
- one decisive figure over many weak figures
- diagrams that clarify mechanisms
- notation-light visuals where possible

## Exercises

Aim for a mix of:
- comprehension
- mechanism tracing
- empirical critique
- research design
- replication or extension
