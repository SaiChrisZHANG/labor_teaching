:::{table} Week 11 worker-policy toolkit map
:label: tbl:week11-policy-toolkit

| Policy family | Primary worker-side margin | Secondary margins | Main research challenge |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| In-work subsidies and earnings credits | participation | hours, earnings, filing, take-up | separating incentives from knowledge and filing behavior |
| Unemployment insurance | search, nonemployment duration | reemployment wage, consumption smoothing, take-up | insurance vs incentive interpretation |
| Job-search assistance | search intensity and information | match quality, duration, reemployment | short-run gains versus medium-run persistence |
| Training and job-readiness programs | human capital and employability | search, occupational mobility, earnings growth | dynamic effects versus short-run placement |
| Childcare and family-support policy | time allocation and participation | fertility, hours, household incidence | within-household incidence and equilibrium care prices |
| Disability and retirement policy | participation and exit timing | household labor supply, savings, reapplication | dynamic incentives, judge IV, bunching, and selection |
| Take-up design and administrative simplification | program reach | salience, equity, measured treatment intensity | eligibility is not the same as policy exposure |
