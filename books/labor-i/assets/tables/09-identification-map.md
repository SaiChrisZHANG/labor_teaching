:::{table} Identification map for empirical discrimination designs
:label: tbl:disc-identification-map

| Design | What varies? | Main estimand | Strength | Limitation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Oaxaca--Blinder / regression decomposition | conditioning set and coefficient structure | descriptive explained vs unexplained gap | transparent and easy to implement | not fully causal; sensitive to controls and functional form |
| Correspondence / audit study | signaled identity holding credentials fixed | direct treatment effect at callback/contact margin | clean treatment variation | narrow margin; equilibrium consequences not fully observed |
| Paired audits / within-firm comparisons | matched testers or matched applicants | treatment within common opportunity set | controls for some demand heterogeneity | costly, limited external validity, tester effects |
| Quasi-experimental information or policy design | information sets, monitoring, or legal environment | causal change in discriminatory treatment or belief updating | broader institutional insight | design-specific interpretation |
| Structural / equilibrium model | primitives generating wages, task choice, and sorting | inferred discrimination wedge in equilibrium | connects treatment to allocation and welfare | strong modeling assumptions |

:::
