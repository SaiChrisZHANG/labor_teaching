
:::{table} Where Labor I ends and Labor II begins
:label: tbl:week13-l1-l2-bridge
| Labor I worker-side question | Natural Labor II extension |
| --- | --- |
| How elastic is labor supply? | How do firms set schedules, wages, and hiring margins when labor supply is elastic? |
| How do workers value amenities? | How do firms provide, price, and compete over amenities in equilibrium? |
| How do workers acquire skill? | How do firms shape training, task assignment, and internal mobility? |
| How does inequality arise across workers? | How much comes from firm heterogeneity, wage-setting, and labor-market power? |
| How does discrimination affect worker outcomes? | How do firms, occupations, search, and institutions propagate or attenuate discrimination? |
| How do workers respond to policy? | How do firms, vacancies, and wages adjust when policy changes worker incentives? |
| How do behavioral frictions distort worker choices? | How do employers design contracts or exploit / offset those frictions? |
