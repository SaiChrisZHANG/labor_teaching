:::{table} Elasticity taxonomy for static labor supply
:label: tbl:week2-elasticity-taxonomy
| Elasticity object | Main margin | Common empirical design | Main interpretation risk |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Uncompensated intensive-margin elasticity | Hours conditional on work | Tax reforms, wage variation, panel estimates | Mixes income and substitution effects |
| Compensated intensive-margin elasticity | Hours conditional on work | Structural or sufficient-statistics adjustments | Hard to recover without additional assumptions |
| Extensive-margin participation elasticity | Work / nonwork choice | EITC reforms, welfare reforms, threshold variation | Sensitive to fixed costs, childcare, and benefit loss |
| Taxable-income elasticity | Reported earnings / taxable income | Kink or bunching designs | May reflect evasion, avoidance, or timing rather than real labor supply |
| Macro labor-supply elasticity | Aggregate hours / participation | Cross-regime comparisons, long-run reforms | Captures adjustment, equilibrium, and institution responses |
:::
