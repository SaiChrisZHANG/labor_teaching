:::{table} Week 7 empirical design map
:label: tbl:week7-design-map

| Design | Main identifying object | Strength | Principal threat |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hedonic wage regression | cross-sectional wage-amenity slope | simple and often broad in scope | omitted worker tastes, omitted firm heterogeneity, multidimensional amenities |
| Risk-premium / VSL studies | wage compensation for fatality or injury risk | classic equilibrium application with policy relevance | risk measurement, selection, union/regulation confounds |
| Discrete-choice / conjoint experiment | willingness to pay for explicit attributes | directly estimates marginal valuations of named amenities | external validity, attention, design dependence |
| Revealed-preference worker-flow methods | latent firm/job value from job-to-job choices | uses actual choices rather than stated preferences | requires rich mobility data and stronger structure |
| Working-conditions stated-preference bundle | valuation of broad job-quality bundles | connects amenities to inequality and total compensation | measurement/comparability across job attributes |
:::
