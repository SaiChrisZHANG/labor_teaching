# Transfer note

The transfer exercise uses a local synthetic panel in the spirit of [@klevenLandaisSogaard2019].

The point is not to recreate the article in full detail. The point is to keep students inside the same family-labor empirical family while changing the object:

- the reproduction step emphasizes a causal fertility margin induced by IVF treatment success;
- the transfer step emphasizes a dynamic post-birth path in earnings, hours, and participation;
- the comparison clarifies why "effect of children" and "child penalty path" are related but not identical objects.

That makes the transfer exercise a natural bridge from local causal fertility effects to dynamic gender-inequality accounting.
