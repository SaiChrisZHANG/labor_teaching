## Synthetic transfer design

The transfer file records a synthetic person-year panel around first birth with:

- event time from five years before first birth to ten years after;
- separate mother and father outcome paths;
- earnings, hours, and participation outcomes that diverge after birth;
- stable pre-trends by construction so students can focus on post-birth dynamics.

The intended exercise is to compare outcome paths around the birth event and summarize the persistent mother-father gap that opens afterward.

## Interpretation warning

This transfer file supports bounded event-study analysis only. It does not reproduce administrative population coverage, cohort decomposition, or the full accounting framework of the published article.
