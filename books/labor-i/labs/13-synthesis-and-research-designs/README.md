# Week 13 Lab: Synthesis and Research Designs

This lab is the bounded research-design studio for Labor I Week 13.

- Canonical handout: `lab.md`

- Primary scripts: `src/build_week13_research_studio_packet.py`, `src/reproduce_week13_anchor_map.py`, `src/propose_week13_extension_memo.py`
