# Source notes

The Week 13 packet is a teaching scaffold, not a paper replication.

It summarizes a small menu of anchor papers that already appear in Labor I:

- Card (1999) on causal returns and identification
- Autor, Katz, and Kearney (2008) on inequality architecture
- Card et al. (2018) on worker--firm wage components
- Neumark (2018) on discrimination design
- DellaVigna (2009) on behavioral wedges and interpretation

Students should treat the generated CSV as a proposal-design input, not as research data.
