# Transfer note

The transfer exercise uses a local synthetic dataset in the spirit of [@stephensYang2014].

The point is not to recreate the article in full detail. The point is to keep students inside the same compulsory-schooling empirical family while changing the trend environment:

- the reproduction step emphasizes a clean compulsory-schooling first stage and a local IV return;
- the transfer step emphasizes how cohort trends and specification choices can move the estimated return even when the instrument looks familiar.

That makes the transfer exercise a natural bridge from "credible instrument" to "credible estimand and trend control."
