## Synthetic transfer design

The transfer file records individual-level synthetic outcomes from a stylized compulsory-schooling setting with:

- birth cohorts around reform windows;
- region groups with different secular wage and schooling trends;
- a law-based instrument that shifts completed schooling;
- wage outcomes generated by both schooling and background trends.

The intended exercise is to compare a pooled IV estimate with a trend-adjusted IV estimate and ask whether the estimated return is robust to the trend environment.

## Interpretation warning

This transfer file supports bounded trend-sensitivity analysis only. It does not reproduce the full legal-history coding, sample construction, or inference strategy of the published article.
