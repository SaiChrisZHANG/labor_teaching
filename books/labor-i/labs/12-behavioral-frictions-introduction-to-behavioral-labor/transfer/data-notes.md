# Transfer Data Notes

## Transfer anchor

- Paper logic: extra work can respond to reciprocity and employer generosity differently than to pure monetary incentives, and the return to the employer need not be the operative worker margin.
- Bounded local file: `transfer/data/della_vigna_gift_exchange_synthetic.csv`
- Teaching outcomes: `extra_work_units`, `productivity_index`, `employer_return_high`

## Interpretation

The transfer file is designed to teach gift-exchange logic and comparison across mechanisms. It estimates causal contrasts in a bounded synthetic setting, not a universal structural parameter for workplace reciprocity.
