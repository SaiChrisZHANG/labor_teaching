# Transfer Data Notes

## Transfer anchor

- Paper logic: reminders and simplified communication can raise EITC take-up by changing information and administrative burden rather than the statutory schedule.
- Bounded local file: `transfer/data/linos_eitc_nudge_synthetic.csv`
- Teaching outcomes: `take_up`, `completed_filing`, `benefit_received_k`

## Interpretation

The transfer file is designed to teach program-delivery logic. It estimates causal delivery effects, not a universal structural elasticity of labor supply.
