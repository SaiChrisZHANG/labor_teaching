# Transfer Data Notes

## Transfer anchor

- Paper logic: migration reform changes labor-market access, with effects scaled by pre-reform exposure.
- Bounded local file: `transfer/data/beerli_open_border_synthetic.csv`
- Teaching outcomes: `ee_reallocation_rate`, `log_firm_employment`, `native_wage_growth`

## Interpretation

The transfer file is designed to teach reform-exposure logic. It estimates reduced-form labor-market responses, not a single structural channel.
