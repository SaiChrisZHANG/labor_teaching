# Source notes

## Primary anchor

- `@attanasioEtAl2020HumanCapital`

## What the bounded teaching file preserves

- a randomized treatment indicator;
- baseline skill differences across children;
- parental investment responses to treatment;
- endline skill gains that depend on both treatment and baseline preparedness.

## What the bounded teaching file does not preserve

- the full production-function estimation stack;
- measurement-system details for latent skills and parental investments;
- exact institutional features of the Colombia intervention;
- publication-quality standard errors or formal inference objects.

## Teaching purpose

The goal is to let students practice the design logic of treatment-induced investment and later skill formation without requiring restricted data access or a heavy structural pipeline.
