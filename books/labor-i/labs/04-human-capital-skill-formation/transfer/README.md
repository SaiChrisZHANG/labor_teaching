# Transfer note

The transfer exercise uses a local synthetic dataset in the spirit of [@walters2015].

The point is not to recreate Head Start in detail. The point is to keep students inside the same empirical family while changing the environment:

- the reproduction step emphasizes treatment-induced investment and child skill;
- the transfer step emphasizes heterogeneity by center quality and input composition.

That makes the transfer exercise a natural bridge from direct intervention effects to the broader question of which inputs inside a program appear most productive.
