## Secondary anchor

- `@walters2015`

## Synthetic transfer design

The transfer file records child-level outcomes from a stylized early-childhood program with:

- a treatment indicator,
- baseline skill,
- center-quality categories,
- classroom-input intensity,
- endline skill and language outcomes.

The intended exercise is to compare treatment gains across center-quality groups and ask whether the treatment effect appears larger where the input environment is stronger.

## Interpretation warning

This transfer file supports bounded descriptive heterogeneity only. It does not identify structural production-function parameters or a full decomposition of center inputs.
