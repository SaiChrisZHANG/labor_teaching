## Theory-to-Applied Bridge

| Economic question | Why reduced-form is limited | Structural contribution | Representative paper |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Should a capital good be replaced now or later? | Static correlations do not recover continuation values | Infers replacement costs and dynamic value of waiting | [@rust1987optimal] |
| How do young people choose school, work, and careers? | One-period treatment effects do not capture path dependence | Models dynamic returns to early choices and future options | [@keane1997career] |
| What does a schooling subsidy do over the lifecycle? | Observed treatment effects may not transport to new regimes | Simulates policy rules and dynamic substitution margins | [@todd2006assessing] |
| How do workers insure wage and employment risk? | Reduced-form estimates cannot separately recover latent risk-sharing objects | Maps observed trajectories to wage risk, employment risk, and insurance | [@low2010wage] |
| What is the career cost of children? | Static comparisons miss dynamic timing and persistence | Quantifies long-run career impacts and intertemporal responses | [@adda2017career] |
