:::{table} Time RD, spatial RD, RKD, and bunching
:label: tbl:local-design-extensions
| Design | Identifying object | Best scenario | Main threat |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Time RD | Local outcome jump around a date | Sharp policy date with highly local comparison | Seasonality, serial correlation, anticipation, concurrent policy changes |
| Event study / DID | Comparison of treated and untreated paths over time | Credible untreated trend path exists | Bad comparisons, staggered-timing bias, spillovers |
| Spatial RD (1D) | Outcome jump across a border, often indexed by signed distance | Institutional border is substantively meaningful and local comparability is plausible | Geographic sorting, non-border confounders |
| Spatial RD (multi-D) | Local contrast around a multidimensional boundary | Rich geographic data and credible local continuity | High-dimensional specification choices and boundary complexity |
| RKD | Local slope change ratio at a kink | Policy changes marginal incentives but not treatment level | Derivative estimation is noisy and assumptions are stronger |
| Bunching at kinks | Excess mass / elasticity around a kink | Sharp schedules, salient incentives, plausible counterfactual density | Model dependence, frictions, imperfect salience |
| Bunching at notches | Discrete avoidance / relocation around a notch | Agents respond strongly to dominated regions | Counterfactual density and optimization assumptions matter a lot |
:::
