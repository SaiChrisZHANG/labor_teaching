## Theory-to-Applied Bridge

| Frontier question | Why standard methods struggle | What the LLM workflow adds | What still requires economic judgment |
|---|---|---|---|
| How do labor-market institutions appear in text-heavy documents? | objects are written before numeric | scalable extraction/classification from contracts, filings, postings | construct validity, benchmark quality, downstream interpretation |
| How can researchers code nuanced categories at scale? | human coding is costly and noisy | synthetic coding/annotation workflows | label protocol, subgroup bias, measurement error consequences |
| Can we pilot experiments before fielding them? | human pilots are slow and expensive | synthetic respondents / simulated agents | transportability from AI behavior to human behavior |
| Can we simulate institutional environments quickly? | building full structural simulation is slow | LLM agent environments and scenario generation | discipline of state/action rules and empirical validation |
| How can research workflows become more productive without becoming opaque? | many steps are labor-intensive | LLMs as assistants and agents for coding/search/orchestration | logging, replication, privacy, audit trails |
