| Theory object | Applied translation | Typical data | Practical implementation challenge |
|---|---|---|---|
| Endogenous treatment choice | Treatment correlated with unobservables | Administrative or survey treatment/outcome data plus instrument source | Distinguishing causal treatment variation from endogenous sorting |
| Exogenous shifter | Institutional rule, distance, judge, shock, or legacy variable | Policy rules, geographic access, quasi-random assignment, shock panels, historical archives | Showing the shifter is truly exogenous for the relevant counterfactual |
| Complier-specific effect | Local response for the margin moved by the instrument | Treatment and instrument variation around the affected margin | Overstating LATE as an average effect for everyone |
| Multiple margins of treatment | Several instruments move different people | Rich administrative or linked data with multiple instrument sources | 2SLS with multiple instruments may obscure who is identified |
| Instrument uncertainty | Weak first stage or partial exclusion concerns | Small samples, noisy instruments, many-IV settings | Need weak-IV robust inference and sensitivity analysis |
