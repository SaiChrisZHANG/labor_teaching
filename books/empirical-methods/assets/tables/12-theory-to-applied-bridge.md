## Theory-to-Applied Bridge

| Paper / setting | Raw modality | Constructed variable | Why the variable matters economically | Key defense |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Hansen et al. | job-posting text | remote-work feasibility | measures work arrangement possibilities by occupation/firm/place | human-labeled training set and cross-context checks |
| Webb | patent text + task text | AI exposure | maps new technology to tasks and occupations | explicit semantic mapping between technology descriptions and task text |
| Hassan et al. | earnings-call transcripts | firm-level epidemic exposure | measures perceived firm shocks before outcomes move | decomposition into demand/supply/uncertainty topics |
| Jean et al. | satellite imagery | poverty proxy | expands measurement to places without dense surveys | validation against survey ground truth |
| Gorodnichenko et al. | central-bank audio | vocal tone | treats audio as an economic information channel | links acoustic features to market responses |
| Haaland et al. | open-ended responses / speech / LLM-assisted coding | motives, narratives, attention | recovers mental models and qualitative content at scale | explicit discussion of annotation and validation choices |
