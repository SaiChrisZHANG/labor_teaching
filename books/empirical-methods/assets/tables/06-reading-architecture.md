### What to read for class discussion
1. Start with [@keane2010structural] to understand what structure is buying.
2. Read [@rust1987optimal] and [@hotz1993conditional] for the basic dynamic-choice toolkit.
3. Read one labor/career application, ideally [@keane1997career] or [@adda2017career], and ask what is latent, what variation disciplines it, and which counterfactuals the model enables.
