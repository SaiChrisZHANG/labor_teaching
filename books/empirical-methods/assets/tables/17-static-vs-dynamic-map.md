## Static vs dynamic / equilibrium map

| Design question | Static spatial model | Dynamic spatial model | Gravity-style block |
|---|---|---|---|
| Main use | Comparative statics and long-run incidence | Transition paths and forward-looking adjustment | Bilateral flows inside broader equilibrium |
| Core margins | Wages, rents, migration, commuting, amenities | Migration timing, housing tenure, adjustment lags | Trade, migration, commuting shares |
| Typical data | Cross-section or long-difference moments | Panels with adjustment dynamics | Bilateral origin-destination matrices |
| Best when | Long-run policy incidence is central | Transition or anticipation is central | Flow frictions are the main object |
| Main caveat | May hide transition costs | Heavy computation and stronger assumptions | Usually incomplete without broader market structure |
