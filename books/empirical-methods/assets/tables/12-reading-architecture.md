## Reading Architecture

| Role | Reading | Why it is here |
|---|---|---|
| Core overview | Gentzkow, Kelly, and Taddy | canonical economics overview of text-as-data logic |
| Core labor application | Hansen et al. | job-posting classification and labor-market measurement |
| Measurement design | Webb | how textual similarity becomes an exposure measure |
| Firm-text application | Hassan et al. | topic extraction and firm-level risk measurement |
| Image application | Jean et al. | image-based proxy construction with ground-truth validation |
| Audio application | Gorodnichenko, Pham, and Talavera | voice features as economically meaningful variables |
| Open-ended / LLM frontier | Haaland et al. | modern economics use of open-ended and LLM-assisted measurement |
| Technical resource | Devlin et al.; He et al.; Baevski et al.; Radford et al. | where to look if students need modality-specific ML detail |
