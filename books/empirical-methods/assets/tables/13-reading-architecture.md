## Reading Architecture

| Role | Reading | Why it is here |
|---|---|---|
| Core validation principles | Gentzkow, Kelly, and Taddy | broad economics framework for measurement and validation |
| Core applied validation example | Hansen et al. | modern economics paper with labeled data and validation logic |
| Open-ended / LLM validation frontier | Haaland et al. | how economists validate qualitative and LLM-assisted measures |
| Image validation example | Jean et al. | classic ground-truth validation of image-derived variables |
| Audio validation example | Gorodnichenko, Pham, and Talavera | validation of vocal features in an economics application |
| Measurement-error warning | Blattman et al.; Bound | why validation matters for downstream causal/descriptive inference |
| Technical resources | Devlin et al.; He et al.; Baevski et al.; Radford et al. | where to look for modality-specific method details |
