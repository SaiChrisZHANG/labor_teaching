| Dimension | Main question | Practical implementation check | Common failure mode |
|---|---|---|---|
| Relevance | Does {math}`Z` move {math}`D`? | Report first-stage coefficient, first-stage fit, and weak-IV diagnostics | Mechanical but economically trivial first stage |
| Independence | Is {math}`Z` as-good-as-random? | Explain assignment rule, timing, routing, or shock source | Non-random sorting into instrument variation |
| Exclusion | Can {math}`Z` affect {math}`Y` except through {math}`D`? | Name the leading direct channels and test/falsify when possible | Geography, institutions, or decision-makers affect many outcomes |
| Monotonicity | Is the sign of the first-stage response one-sided? | Explain why defiers are implausible in this setting | Strategic responses make monotonicity unclear |
| First-stage content | What margin of treatment is being moved? | Describe the compliers substantively | “Strong” first stage with no economic interpretation |
| Inference level | Where does independent variation live? | Cluster at the shock / judge / cohort / instrument level when appropriate | Overly optimistic standard errors |
| Transportability | Does the local margin answer the substantive question? | Describe who the compliers are and whether the margin matters | Overstating a local effect as universal |
| Sensitivity | How fragile is the exclusion story? | Use reduced-form sign checks, alternative controls, or plausibly exogenous bounds | Treating exclusion as solved once 2SLS is reported |
