:::{table} Theory-to-applied bridge for Lecture 1
:label: tbl:theory-applied-bridge
| Theoretical object | Applied translation | Typical empirical move | What readers will ask |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Potential outcomes | What would treated units have looked like without treatment? | Construct untreated comparison group | Why is that comparison credible? |
| Selection bias | Why do treated and untreated differ before treatment? | Write down selection story explicitly | What is the main omitted selection margin? |
| Conditional independence | Which observables block the relevant backdoor paths? | Match/weight/control on pre-treatment covariates | Are the key confounders really observed? |
| Overlap | Is there meaningful support for comparison? | Trim support, diagnose tails, redefine estimand | Are you comparing like with like? |
| ATT vs ATE | Which average effect matters? | Align estimator with policy question | For whom is the result relevant? |
| Robustness | How fragile is the design to hidden bias? | Sensitivity analysis and alternative estimators | Would modest unobservables overturn the result? |
:::
