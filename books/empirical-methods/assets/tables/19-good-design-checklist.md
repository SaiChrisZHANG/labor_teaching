| Design question | What a good paper should answer |
|---|---|
| Network definition | Why are these nodes and edges the economically relevant relationships? |
| Exposure mapping | What treatment or peer-exposure states actually capture the mechanism? |
| Source of variation | Does identification come from randomization, topology, timing, or excluded-peer variation? |
| Interference structure | Who can affect whom, and why is that assumption credible in this setting? |
| Sorting threat | How does the paper address endogenous network or peer-group formation? |
| Inference | Are standard errors valid under dependence, or is dyadic / design-based inference needed? |
| Interpretation | Is the estimand direct, spillover, equilibrium, or a mixture? |
| External validity | Would the estimated peer/spillover effect travel if the network or market scale changed? |
