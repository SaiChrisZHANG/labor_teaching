## Theory-to-applied bridge

| Reduced-form fact | Structural object needed | Typical paper template | Applied payoff |
|---|---|---|---|
| Local wage effect differs across places | Housing supply elasticity / moving costs | Hsieh-Moretti | Incidence and misallocation |
| Employment shock absorbed through commuting | Commuting vs migration costs | Monte-Redding-Rossi-Hansberg | Local labor-demand incidence |
| Urban redevelopment changes welfare unevenly | Amenity and productivity gradients | Ahlfeldt-Redding-Sturm-Wolf | Welfare decomposition |
| Bilateral trade or migration flows shape local outcomes | Flow elasticity / market access | Allen-Arkolakis | Spatial policy counterfactuals |
| Urban transition path matters after shock | Dynamic migration / housing frictions | Greaney-Parkhomenko-Van Nieuwerburgh | Transition dynamics and policy timing |
