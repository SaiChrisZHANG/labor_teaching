# Transfer Data Notes

The transfer data are generated by `src/make_synthetic_data.py`.

The shift-share exercise uses county-sector baseline shares and common sector shocks. Students construct:

```text
Z_l = sum_s w_ls g_s
```

where `w_ls` is a county baseline share and `g_s` is a sector shock.

The exercise is intentionally conservative. A shift-share exposure is a design input, not a valid instrument by construction. Students must diagnose dominant sectors, leave-one-out exposure, geography choice, and whether the identifying assumption is about shares, shocks, or both.
