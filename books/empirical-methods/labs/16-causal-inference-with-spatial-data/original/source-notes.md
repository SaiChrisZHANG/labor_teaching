# Source Notes

The original teaching path is inspired by contiguous-border comparisons in applied economics, especially designs that compare nearby counties on opposite sides of a policy border.

The synthetic data include:

- county-level observations arranged into border pairs;
- coordinates used to construct spatial distances;
- predetermined covariates for balance checks;
- a post-period outcome change;
- pairwise distance links for spatial-inference diagnostics;
- sector shares and sector shocks for the transfer exercise.

These files are generated locally. They are not an official replication package and should not be used for substantive claims.
