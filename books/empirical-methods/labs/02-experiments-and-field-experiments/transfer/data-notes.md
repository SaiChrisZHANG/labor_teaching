# Transfer Data Notes

The transfer dataset is synthetic. It is inspired by labor-market policy experiments where treatment saturation varies across local markets.

Variables include:

- market identifiers and market treatment saturation;
- individual assignment to job-search assistance;
- baseline employability measures;
- realized program take-up;
- employment outcomes;
- untreated-worker exposure to treated competitors in the same market.

The design task is to decide whether the main object is an individual ITT, a treatment-on-receipt contrast, a spillover effect, or a market-level policy effect.
