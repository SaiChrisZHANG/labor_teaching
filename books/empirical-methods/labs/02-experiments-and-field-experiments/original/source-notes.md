# Source Notes

The reproduce path is inspired by Duflo and Saez's retirement-plan seminar field experiment. The teaching dataset keeps the economic structure, not the original data:

- workers are grouped into departments;
- workers have baseline salary, tenure, prior retirement-plan enrollment, and age;
- seminar encouragement is randomly assigned within departments;
- seminar attendance is realized take-up and is not randomized after assignment;
- retirement-plan participation is the outcome;
- coworker encouragement exposure creates a simple spillover channel;
- a small amount of outcome attrition is included to force diagnostic work.

Use this dataset to practice design interpretation. Do not use it to make claims about the original paper's magnitudes.
