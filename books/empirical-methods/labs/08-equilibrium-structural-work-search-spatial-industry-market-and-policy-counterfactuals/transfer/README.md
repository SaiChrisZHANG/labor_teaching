# Transfer teaching path

The transfer path uses deterministic synthetic differentiated-product market data to illustrate a BLP/Nevo-style market-equilibrium counterfactual.

The data are stored in:

```text
transfer/data/market_equilibrium_synthetic.csv
```

The exercise estimates a compact logit demand equation, recovers implied markups under a simple pricing rule, and simulates a marginal-cost shock. It is designed to compare spatial incidence with market-equilibrium pass-through.
