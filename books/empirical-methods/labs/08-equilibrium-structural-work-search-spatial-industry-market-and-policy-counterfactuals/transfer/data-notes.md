# Transfer data notes

The transfer data contain product-market observations with market size, price, market share, outside share, quality, cost shifters, labor intensity, and automation exposure.

The transfer script:

- estimates a compact logit demand equation;
- computes own-price elasticities and implied markups;
- recovers marginal costs using a simple pricing condition;
- applies a marginal-cost shock to high-exposure products;
- resolves prices and shares under the new cost environment;
- reports pass-through and labor-demand incidence.

This is a teaching path rather than a BLP or Nevo replication. A publishable paper would need credible instruments, richer substitution patterns, product ownership, supply-side cost shifters, uncertainty, and validation against external moments.
