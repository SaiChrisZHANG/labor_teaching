# Source Notes

This lab is inspired by workplace peer-effects research, especially Cornelissen, Dustmann, and Schonberg [@cornelissenPeerEffectsWorkplace2017]. The repository does not ship official replication materials for that paper.

The teaching data are synthetic. They are designed to make the following objects visible:

- teams with different treatment saturation rates;
- directed coworker-neighbor links inside teams;
- leave-one-out team peer measures;
- graph-neighbor treatment exposure;
- contemporaneous peer outcomes that are reflection-prone;
- outside-team contact counts that make partial interference a diagnostic question.

The synthetic data should be used only as a methods exercise. They do not estimate the magnitude of real workplace peer effects.
