# Source notes

## Verified source anchor

- **Article:** Friebel, Guido, Matthias Heinz, Miriam Krueger, and Nikolay Zubanov. 2017. *Team Incentives and Performance: Evidence from a Retail Chain*. *American Economic Review* 107(8): 2168--2203.

## Why this paper fits Week 3

This paper is a strong anchor for the Week 3 lab because it makes students identify an internal firm treatment clearly. The incentive change happens inside one organization, the observed margins are team performance and profits, and the interpretation naturally turns on effort, complementarities, and implementation rather than on general-equilibrium labor-market forces.

## Teaching note

The local lab path is synthetic and bounded. It preserves the design logic of a team-incentive experiment while avoiding confidential personnel records. The pedagogical goal is to make the Week 3 objects runnable, not to ship a full firm replication package.
