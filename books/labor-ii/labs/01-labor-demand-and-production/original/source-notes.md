# Source notes

## Verified source anchor

- **Article:** Beaudry, Paul, David A. Green, and Ben M. Sand. 2018. *In Search of Labor Demand*. *American Economic Review* 108(9): 2714--2757.

## Why this paper fits Week 1

This paper is a strong anchor for the opening Labor II lab because it makes students identify the source of demand variation rather than talk about labor demand as a single textbook curve. The Week 1 chapter's central distinction between conditional and total labor demand becomes concrete once local demand shifts are mapped into employment and wage responses.

## Teaching note

The local lab path is synthetic and bounded. It preserves the design logic of a city-industry demand exercise while avoiding any confidential or large external files. The pedagogical goal is to make the Week 1 objects runnable, not to ship a full paper replication.
