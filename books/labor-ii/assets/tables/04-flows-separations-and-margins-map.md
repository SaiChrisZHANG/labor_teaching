:::{table} Flows, separations, and margins map for Week 4
:label: tbl:flows-separations-week4

| Margin | Worker-side interpretation | Firm-side interpretation | Main theory block | Main empirical challenge |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| E → U | Job loss, layoff, quit into unemployment | Separation decision, job destruction, retention failure | Flow unemployment, endogenous destruction | Distinguishing quits, layoffs, and displacement |
| U → E | Reemployment | Vacancy filling from nonemployment pool | Matching, search effort, reservation wages | Composition of searchers and vacancies |
| E → E | Upward or lateral mobility without unemployment | Poaching, outside offers, retention pressure | On-the-job search, job ladders | Need linked worker-firm or panel flow data |
| Stable employment with internal turnover | Replacement hiring, reorganization | Churning, restructuring, personnel adjustment | Turnover and excess reallocation | Net employment masks gross flows |
| Long unemployment spell | Search friction, low hazard, or dynamic selection | Reduced outside-option pressure on firms | Duration dependence, beliefs, scarring | Causal duration effects vs heterogeneity |
| Reemployment wage / job quality | Match quality after nonemployment | Selection into lower- or higher-quality firms | Reservation wages, selective hiring, scarring | Past wage, firm quality, and occupation choice are jointly determined |
