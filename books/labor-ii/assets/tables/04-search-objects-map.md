:::{table} Search objects map for Week 4
:label: tbl:search-objects-week4

| Object | Definition | Typical data source | Why it matters | Common confusion to avoid |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Job-finding rate | Hazard from unemployment to employment | CPS flows, admin records | Governs outflow from unemployment | Not the same as vacancy-filling |
| Separation rate | Hazard from employment to unemployment or nonemployment | CPS flows, employer records | Governs inflow into unemployment | Not all separations are layoffs |
| Vacancy-filling rate | Hazard from vacancy to match | JOLTS, firm vacancy data | Captures recruiting congestion | Not directly observable from worker data |
| Job-to-job transition rate | Hazard from one employer to another without unemployment | Matched employer-employee data, CPS supplements | Core object for job ladders and sorting | Not captured by unemployment-only models |
| Churning / excess worker reallocation | Worker flows beyond net employment changes | Employer-level administrative data | Separates internal turnover from net job creation | Not the same as job destruction |
| Unemployment duration gradient | Relationship between spell length and later outcomes or hazards | Survey panels, admin data, search apps | Central to causal vs selection debates | Duration dependence need not be causal |

:::
