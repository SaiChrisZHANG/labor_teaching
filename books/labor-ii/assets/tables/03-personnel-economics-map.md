:::{table} Personnel economics map for Week 3
:label: tbl:personnel-map-week3

| Object of study | Canonical question | Main theoretical lens | Typical data / design | Bridge to the rest of Labor II |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Incentives and compensation | How should firms pay workers when effort is imperfectly observed? | Agency, risk-incentive tradeoff, piece rates, bonus pay | Firm experiments, pay-plan changes, productivity records | Links the firm's internal problem to later wage-setting and retention questions |
| Multitasking and evaluation | What happens when measured output is an incomplete proxy for value? | Multitasking, subjective evaluation, relational incentives | Performance dashboards, supervisor ratings, task-level output | Helps explain why internal organization matters even when markets are competitive |
| Promotions and internal labor markets | How should firms allocate workers across roles and career ladders? | Assignment, tournaments, career concerns, Peter Principle | Personnel records, promotion events, manager-switch designs | Bridges internal organization to turnover, quits, and search in Week 4 |
| Managers and supervision | What do managers do, and how much do they matter for productivity and retention? | Manager value added, coaching, monitoring, information transmission | Matched manager-worker panels, reassignment shocks, surveys | Foreshadows later questions about firms, bargaining, and institutions |
| Peers and teams | When does coworker composition affect effort, learning, or output? | Peer pressure, knowledge spillovers, complementarities, free-riding | Shift-level data, team experiments, network designs | Connects internal organization to productivity and inequality across firms |
| Practices and workplace rules | Which organizational practices change worker outcomes and firm performance? | Management practices, communication, voice, flexibility, training | Surveys, policy thresholds, phased rollouts, digital traces | Links firm organization to modern policy and technology questions |
