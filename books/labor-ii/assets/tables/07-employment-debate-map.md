:::{table} Employment-debate map for minimum wages
:label: tbl:mw-debate-map

| Debate object | Representative papers | Typical data | Margin identified | Main caveat for interpretation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fast-food case studies | `@cardKrueger1994` | Establishment surveys | Employment in treated sector | Narrow geography and sector; external validity debates |
| National panel / traditional review | `@neumarkWascher2007` | State-year panels | Broader employment elasticities | Vulnerable to trend heterogeneity and specification sensitivity |
| Border discontinuity designs | `@dubeLesterReich2010` | Contiguous county pairs + QCEW | Local employment and earnings in low-wage sectors | Stronger local controls, but still local and sector-specific |
| Threshold/job-distribution designs | `@cengizDubeLindnerZipperer2019` | Administrative wage bins / job counts | Jobs below and around the new floor | Requires careful handling of reclassification and spillovers |
| City-specific large hikes | `@jardimEtAl2022` | City-level admin data + synthetic controls | Hours, low-wage jobs, retention | Large local hikes may mix treatment effects with special local conditions |
| Global / national reform evidence | `@engbomMoser2022`; `@dubeLindner2024` | Country-wide admin + survey data | Wages, inequality, employment, reallocation | Institutions differ; comparability across countries is limited |
