# Source Notes

The reproduction exercise is inspired by Dell's historical boundary design for the mining mita [@dell2010mita]. The data are synthetic and are intended only to teach the difference between historical exposure, mechanism evidence, and modern labor outcomes.

The challenge anchor is Naidu and Yuchtman's coercive contract-enforcement setting [@naiduYuchtman2013coercive]. Optional extensions point to serfdom abolition and Reconstruction-era education [@markevichZhuravskaya2018serfdom; @jonesSchmick2025reconstruction].
