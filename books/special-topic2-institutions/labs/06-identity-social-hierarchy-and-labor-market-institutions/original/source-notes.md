# Source Notes

This lab ships synthetic data only. The rows are designed for teaching the logic of:

- resume-audit callback comparisons;
- immigrant credential and local-experience screening;
- network and occupation-persistence diagnostics;
- contact and integration experiments;
- public-rule and local-governance shocks;
- macro talent-misallocation frameworks.

No confidential applications, personnel records, caste-network microdata, public employment files, or structural-estimation inputs are required.
