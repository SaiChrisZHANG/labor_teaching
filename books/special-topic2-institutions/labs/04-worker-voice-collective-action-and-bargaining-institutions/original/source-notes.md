# Source Notes

The Week 4 lab is inspired by the empirical objects in [@dinardoLee2004] and [@frandsen2021], but it does not use official replication data.

The synthetic data are designed to teach:

- close certification-election comparisons around a majority threshold;
- the difference between establishment-level and worker-level outcomes;
- organization formation as a margin distinct from wage effects;
- the limits of direct treatment estimates when spillovers and uncovered workers matter.
