# Organization formation and coverage map

| Margin | Worker side | Employer / legal side | Research questions |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Demand for representation | Desire for insurance, voice, fairness, bargaining power | Employer opposition, anti-union campaigns, organizing costs | When do workers want unions? |
| Certification and recognition | Voting, organizing, mobilization | Legal thresholds, election rules, recognition law | How do institutions shape union formation? |
| Labor-market tightness | Outside options and retaliation risk | Employer power and replacement options | Does tightness raise or lower organizing? |
| Coverage vs membership | Membership may be low while coverage is high | Extension rules, sectoral agreements, legal enforcement | Which object matters more for wage-setting? |
| Representation form | Union, works council, codetermination, informal organization | Firm size rules, corporate form, consultation requirements | Which form of voice emerges and why? |
