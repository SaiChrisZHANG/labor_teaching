## Frontier and reading map

| Frontier theme | Why it matters for labor economics | Good starting papers | Open questions |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Norm change and labor-market transition | policies and technology may shift conduct norms faster than formal institutions | [@jayachandran2021]; [@guisoSapienzaZingales2006] | when do conduct norms change and when do they persist? |
| Acceptable workplaces and job design | firms may unintentionally reinforce norms via job framing or contract design | [@floryLeibbrandtList2015]; [@delfino2024] | how should firms redesign recruitment if norms distort entry? |
| Networking norms and career advancement | advancement may depend on informal norms before formal promotion rules bind | [@cullenPerezTruglia2023] | how much of leadership gaps reflect norms vs structural hierarchy? |
| Community norms and local labor supply | local labor-market equilibrium may depend on norms around effort, search, and wages | [@brezaKaurKrishnaswamy2019]; [@clark2003] | when do local norms create aggregate labor-market distortions? |
| Religion and acceptable work | religion may reshape labor supply and entrepreneurship without legal exclusion | [@carvalho2013] | how do religious norms interact with changing local labor demand? |
