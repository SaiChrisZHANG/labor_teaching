## Historical data sources map

| Data source | What it measures well | Typical labor use | Main limitation | Examples |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Historical census and linked census data | occupation, literacy, earnings proxies, migration, household structure | mobility, intergenerational persistence, occupational upgrading, inequality | linkage bias; sparse labor-income detail in some periods | linked census projects, IPUMS, complete-count census |
| School and teacher archives | educational access, local school supply, schooling quality proxies | skill formation, later occupational standing, racial or regional gaps | often uneven coverage across space/time | [@jonesSchmick2025reconstruction] |
| Court and labor-contract records | enforcement, breach, quitting, coercion, disputes | employer power, labor mobility, contract enforcement | surviving records may be selective | [@naiduYuchtman2013coercive] |
| Tax, cadastral, and land records | land concentration, tenancy, local fiscal capacity | land institutions, employer dominance, inequality persistence | difficult harmonization over time | [@markevichZhuravskaya2018serfdom] |
| GIS layers and historical maps | historical boundaries, roads, schools, mines, plantations, rail | border designs, market access, public-goods channels | georeferencing and boundary harmonization | [@dell2010mita] |
| War/conflict and military data | mobilization, defense spending, destruction, occupation, veteran status | labor-demand shocks, discrimination, migration, local reallocation | war affects many outcomes simultaneously | [@aizerBooneLlerasMuneyVogel2020wwii]; [@ferrara2023wars] |
| Digitized newspapers and legal texts | hiring discourse, strikes, legal awareness, policy implementation | worker knowledge, employer discourse, institutional implementation | OCR error, selective reporting |
| Parish/church/registry records | fertility, marriage, lineage, location histories, social categories | family transmission, lineage, migration, community persistence | linkage complexity; local scope |
| Firm and payroll archives | employment, tasks, wages, promotions, separations | within-firm persistence, employer strategies, organizational change | confidentiality and survivorship bias |
