| Design | What it identifies best | Typical labor outcomes | Main limitation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Legal adoption / repeal | law on the books | employment, wages, turnover, litigation-sensitive margins | does not isolate enforcement |
| Court-made legal variation | dismissal protection / contract doctrines | employment, separations, hiring | legal change may be narrow in scope |
| Inspector access / allocation | enforcement intensity | compliance, formalization, wages, benefits | may not capture worker knowledge |
| Worker-information RCTs | knowledge / salience of rights | claims, job creation, take-up of legal protections | does not identify enforcement capacity |
| Comparative institutional indices | cross-country legal environment | broad labor-market institutions and rights | coarse, sometimes weak on implementation |
| Matched employer–employee data | incidence and sorting under enforcement | wages, benefits, worker flows, firm adjustment | often setting-specific and data-intensive |
