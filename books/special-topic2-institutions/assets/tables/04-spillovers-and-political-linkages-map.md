# Spillovers and political linkages map

| Channel | Labor-market effect | Political effect / feedback | Example questions |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Threat effects | Nonunion employers raise wages or improve conditions to deter organization | Can weaken or strengthen future organizing incentives | How far do union effects extend beyond covered workers? |
| Coverage spillovers | Bargaining norms affect uncovered workers and wage structure | May change broader coalitional politics around labor rules | Do unions compress wages economy-wide? |
| Worker mobilization | Unions help workers coordinate around workplace issues | Unions may increase turnout, participation, and campaign contact | Do unions change voting and civic engagement? |
| Politics to labor institutions | RTW laws, bargaining rights, strike law, public-sector rules | Political institutions reshape organizing capacity and coverage | How does politics feed back into worker power? |
| Governance spillovers | Worker voice changes retention, trust, and information-sharing | May alter broader views about workplace democracy and reform | Can representation institutions diffuse beyond organized sectors? |
