## Research design template

| Element | Question to answer | Typical failure | What makes it persuasive |
|---|---|---|---|
| Labor outcome | What labor margin is actually being explained? | Outcome is too broad or only loosely labor-related | Outcome maps directly to work, wages, bargaining, mobility, allocation, or protection |
| Institutional mechanism | What institutional channel is doing the work? | Institutional detail without mechanism | One mechanism is clearly foregrounded |
| Counterfactual | Compared with what? | No credible comparison group | Comparison matches the mechanism |
| Data | What data actually observe the labor margin and the institution? | Data are interesting but not mechanism-relevant | Data line up with the theoretical object |
| Design | What creates identifying variation? | Story outruns design | Variation is explicit, transparent, and well defended |
| Portability | Why should outsiders care? | Setting novelty substitutes for relevance | Mechanism-first framing and clear boundary conditions |
| Welfare / distribution | What normative object matters? | Results stop at average treatment effects | Distributional and welfare implications are explicit |
