# Identification and frontier map

| Design / method | What it identifies | Canonical papers | Frontier directions |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Close-election RD | Causal effect of new unionization on organized units | [@dinardoLee2004]; [@frandsen2021] | Heterogeneity by market power, occupation, and firm type |
| Matched employer-employee panels | Worker-level career, payroll, turnover, and firm response margins | [@frandsen2021] | Longer-run careers, worker sorting, and inequality dynamics |
| Survey / field evidence on organizing demand | Worker demand for representation and how conditions affect organizing | [@pezoldJagerNuss2023] | New forms of representation, online organizing, platform work |
| Governance / representation reforms | Effects of worker voice beyond traditional wage bargaining | [@harjuJagerSchoefer2021]; [@jagerSchoeferHeining2022] | Information sharing, AI monitoring, and firm governance |
| Distributional decompositions | Direct and spillover effects on uncovered workers and inequality | [@fortinLemieuxLloyd2021] | Coverage institutions, nonunion norms, and wage compression |
| Policy and political reforms | Political feedback into unions and unions into politics | [@feigenbaumHertelFernandezWilliamson2018]; [@kaplanNaidu2024] | Union effects on turnout, policy coalitions, and democratic participation |
