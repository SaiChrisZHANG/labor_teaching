## Category and hierarchy map

| Social category | What makes it a labor-market institution? | Main labor margins | Representative papers |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | employer beliefs, segregation, public rules, residential sorting | callbacks, hiring, occupation access, public employment | [@bertrandMullainathan2004]; [@anejaXu2020]; [@hsiehHurstJonesKlenow2019] |
| Caste | network closure, occupation boundaries, social insurance, contact institutions | schooling choice, occupation persistence, mobility, collaboration | [@munshiRosenzweig2006]; [@lowe2021] |
| Migrant status | credential recognition, name/accent screening, legal status, information barriers | callbacks, occupational downgrading, wage loss, mobility | [@oreopoulos2011] |
| Gender as hierarchy | restricted access to occupations and leadership, not just role expectations | occupational access, promotions, talent allocation | [@hsiehHurstJonesKlenow2019] |
| Ethnicity / religion | network closure, local governance, group protection or exclusion | referrals, local access, hiring, promotion | [@darity2022] |
