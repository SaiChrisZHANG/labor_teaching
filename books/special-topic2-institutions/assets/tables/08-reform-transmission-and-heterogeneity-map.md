:::{table} Reform transmission and heterogeneity map
:label: tbl:reform-transmission-map

| Stage | Question | Typical heterogeneity |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Legal change | What changed on paper? | statutory scope, exemptions, rollout timing |
| Implementation | Was the reform enforced or administered effectively? | state capacity, inspector staffing, local government quality |
| Knowledge / beliefs | Did workers and firms understand the reform? | literacy, legal awareness, trust, access to information |
| Exposure | Which workers and firms were actually affected? | firm size, contract type, sector, informality, worker type |
| Firm adaptation | How did firms respond? | compliance, evasion, automation, outsourcing, wage compression, menu redesign |
| Worker response | How did workers react? | labor supply, mobility, claims, search behavior, bargaining, take-up |
| Equilibrium adjustment | What changed in the broader labor market? | spillovers, prices, market composition, uncovered workers, inequality |

:::
