:::{table} Reform taxonomy for labor-institutions research
:label: tbl:reform-taxonomy-map

| Reform family | Typical labor margin | Why effects differ |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Dismissal / employment protection reform | firing, turnover, hiring composition, contract choice | firm size, legal coverage, product-market pressure, enforcement |
| Enforcement expansion | formality, compliance, wages, employment, off-the-books hiring | local state capacity, inspector intensity, worker knowledge, evasion margins |
| Legal-information reform | take-up of rights, bargaining, claims, hiring | baseline legal ignorance, credibility, worker literacy, firm response |
| Formalization/compliance reform | registration, payroll reporting, contract form, firm scale | private benefits of formality, tax burden, enforcement threat, worker demand |
| Equal-opportunity / procedural reform | hiring, promotion, wage gaps, bargaining | occupation structure, evaluator discretion, transparency, outside options |
| Transparency reform | wage setting, bargaining, pay gaps, quits | disclosure scope, worker comparison, employer bargaining response |
| Administrative / service delivery reform | matching, take-up, job search, benefit transitions | agency quality, local rollout, worker trust, employer engagement |

:::
