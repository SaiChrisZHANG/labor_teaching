# Transfer Exercise

Use the reproduced profile and diagnosis table to design one frontier study:

- treatment-access shock;
- waiting-time shock;
- job-loss event study;
- pandemic or workplace reorganization shock;
- disclosure or destigmatization intervention;
- structural job-search model with mental health and job quality.

Keep the labor margin visible. Name the mental-health measure, labor outcome, identifying variation, main threat, and welfare object.
