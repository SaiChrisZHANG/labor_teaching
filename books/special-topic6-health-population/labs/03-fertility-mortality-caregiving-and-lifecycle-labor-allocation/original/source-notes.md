# Source Notes

This lab is conceptually anchored in Fadlon and Nielsen on family labor-supply responses to severe health shocks and Ameriks and coauthors on flexible late-life work. The local files are synthetic and are designed only to teach lifecycle labor-allocation design logic.

The synthetic data make six channels visible:

- income replacement;
- care-time demand;
- own work-capacity loss;
- pension and benefit incentives;
- job flexibility and bridge-job transitions;
- dynamic selection and anticipation.
