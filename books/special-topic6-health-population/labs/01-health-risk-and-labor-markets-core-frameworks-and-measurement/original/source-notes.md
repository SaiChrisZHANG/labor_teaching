# Source Notes

The lab is anchored conceptually in:

- Blundell, Britton, Costa Dias, and French on health measurement and employment near retirement.
- Bound on self-reported versus objective health measures in retirement models.

The local smoke path does not use confidential survey, administrative, biomarker, retirement, or disability data. It creates synthetic teaching data with named measurement objects so students can practice interpretation without relying on replication availability.
