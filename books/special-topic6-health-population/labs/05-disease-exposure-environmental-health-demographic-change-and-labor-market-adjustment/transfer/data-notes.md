# Transfer Data Notes

Transfer settings may require mortality records, vital statistics, historical census data, public-health archives, disease surveillance, administrative earnings, payroll records, UI records, firm output, pollution monitors, weather or satellite exposure data, migration records, commuting flows, pension records, or matched employer-employee data. The Week 5 lab does not provide those data. It asks students to specify which data would be needed and why the design would or would not be credible.
