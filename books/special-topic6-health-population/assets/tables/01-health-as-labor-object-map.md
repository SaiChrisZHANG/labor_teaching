## Health as a labor-market object

| Dimension | Labor interpretation | Typical empirical proxy | Main concern |
|---|---|---|---|
| Work capacity | Ability to sustain hours/tasks | Self-reported limitations, diagnoses, disability status | Proxy may not match actual capacity |
| Productivity | Output conditional on working | Employer records, absenteeism, performance, occupational demands | Firm-side response often unobserved |
| Risk | Probability of future impairment, exit, mortality | Mortality risk, acute shocks, chronic conditions | Dynamic selection and anticipation |
| Feasible set | Which jobs/schedules are tolerable | Occupation switches, exits from strenuous work, commuting tolerance | Unobserved adaptation margins |
| Welfare | Real value of work net of pain/stress/risk | Hours, wages, amenities, treatment access | Wage alone may mismeasure welfare |
