## Aging, pensions, and global evidence

| Theme | Labor question | Representative settings / evidence | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|---|
| Retirement-age rules | Do incentives move labor-force attachment at older ages? | pension reforms, full retirement age changes | identifies policy vs preference margins |
| Flexible late-life jobs | Would older workers stay employed if jobs were redesigned? | survey-based preference elicitation, late-career job design | shows labor-demand constraints, not only worker supply |
| Post-retirement work | Is retirement an exit or a transition into new work arrangements? | bridge jobs, part-time work, rehiring, informal work | changes welfare interpretation of retirement |
| Aging societies | How do demographic shifts alter labor supply and care burdens? | OECD, Europe, East Asia, cross-country retirement evidence | links demographic change to labor-market design |
