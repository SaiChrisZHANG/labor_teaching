| Question | Strong answer looks like | Common weak answer |
|---|---|---|
| Why is this a labor paper? | identifies a labor margin, institution, or welfare object | treats work only as an outcome afterthought |
| Why should others care? | mechanism generalizes beyond one disease/place/program | relevance rests only on one special setting |
| What is the counterfactual? | timing, exposure, and comparison group are explicit | comparison group is vague or endogenous |
| What makes the design credible? | addresses measurement, selection, and dynamic response | assumes self-reports or diagnosis are exogenous |
| What is the welfare object? | explains whether wages/employment are sufficient | ignores hidden harms, flexibility, or health itself |
