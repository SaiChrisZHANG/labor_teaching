## Week 1 field map

| Later week object | How Week 1 prepares it |
|---|---|
| Disability and chronic conditions | Separates health from disability-program status and clarifies why the labor margin must be specified |
| Fertility, mortality, caregiving | Shows that health/population shocks affect lifecycle labor allocation through risk and feasibility |
| Mental health and workplace productivity | Makes clear that latent conditions and mental health require distinct measurement and welfare interpretation |
| Disease / environment / demographics | Sets up health as both individual heterogeneity and aggregate labor-market condition |
| Final synthesis | Establishes the template: labor object → health object → design problem → welfare interpretation |
