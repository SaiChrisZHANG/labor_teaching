### Disability and chronic-conditions mechanisms map

| Margin | Main mechanism | Typical outcomes | Key papers |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Work capacity | Direct limits on tasks, stamina, cognition, or scheduling tolerance | Employment, hours, occupation, productivity | [@meyerMok2019; @collischonHiesingerPohlan2023] |
| Program interaction | Disability definitions, benefit rules, reassessments, return-to-work incentives | Labor-force attachment, benefit take-up, earnings | [@autorDuggan2003disabilityRolls; @frenchSong2014; @zaresani2018] |
| Accommodation | Modified tasks, schedules, assistive technology, leave flexibility | Retention, delayed exit, SSDI claiming | [@hillMaestasMullen2016] |
| Sorting and amenities | Moves toward safer/flexible jobs or away from incompatible jobs | Occupational downgrading, job lock, amenities valuation | [@madrian1994; @ameriSchurAdyaBentleyEtAl2018] |
| Dynamic scarring | Persistent income loss and reduced career progression after onset | Long-run earnings, income, consumption | [@meyerMok2019; @dobkinFinkelsteinKluenderNotowidigdo2018hospital] |
