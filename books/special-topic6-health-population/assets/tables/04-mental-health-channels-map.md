| Channel | Labor margin | Typical measures | Key challenge |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Symptoms / distress | Attendance, effort, search | PHQ-9, GHQ, CES-D, K6 | Reporting bias |
| Diagnosis / treatment | Retention, accommodations, job choice | Diagnoses, visits, prescriptions | Access and stigma |
| Work stress | Productivity, quits, promotion | Job strain, control, schedules | Reverse causality |
| Meaning / identity at work | Well-being, retention, effort | Surveys, matched workplace data | Hard to separate from job quality |
| Severe shocks | Exit, disability, long-run wages | Job loss, family shocks, pandemic shocks | Dynamic selection |
