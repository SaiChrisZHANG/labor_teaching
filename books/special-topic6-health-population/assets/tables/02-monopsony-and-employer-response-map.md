| Mechanism | Why it creates employer power | Labor implication |
|---|---|---|
| Accommodation scarcity | Few firms offer feasible jobs | Lower outside options, lower quits elasticity |
| Health insurance / benefits lock | Benefits tied to employment match | Reduced mobility and wage bargaining |
| Flexible scheduling dependence | Workers need rare schedule amenities | Higher employer-specific labor supply |
| Productivity uncertainty | Employers hold private evaluation power | Stronger screening and wage-setting leverage |
| Hiring discrimination under uncertainty | Outside offers fall | Monopsony-like wage suppression and slower mobility |
