| Workplace feature | Mental-health relevance | Labor relevance | Example research angle |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Autonomy / control | Reduces stress, increases resilience | Retention, productivity | Flexible work and psychological well-being |
| Monitoring / surveillance | Can raise stress or clarity | Effort, quits, welfare | Monitoring technologies and stress |
| Mission / values alignment | Shapes meaning and accomplishment | Motivation, turnover | Identity and employee mental health |
| Social environment | Support vs conflict | Team productivity, absenteeism | Peer interactions and mental strain |
| Schedule volatility | Sleep, anxiety, caregiving stress | Hours, labor supply, exits | Schedule predictability policies |
