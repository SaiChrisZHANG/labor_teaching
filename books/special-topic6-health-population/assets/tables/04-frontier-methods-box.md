| Design family | What it can identify | Typical data | Main caveat |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Treatment access / waiting-time shocks | Effect of treatment or delayed treatment on work | Claims, treatment records, earnings | External validity and treatment composition |
| Job-loss / workplace shocks | Effect of work changes on mental health | Admin panels, layoffs, surveys | Shock bundles many mechanisms |
| Pandemic / remote-work shocks | Stress, isolation, mental-health effects of work organization | Time use, surveys, admin data | Multi-channel environment |
| Structural job-search models | Two-way interactions between work and mental health | Panels + occupational stress measures | Strong assumptions |
| Twin / sibling / genetic designs | Partially address endogeneity of underlying health | Linked family/genetic data | Interpretation and exclusion restrictions are delicate |
