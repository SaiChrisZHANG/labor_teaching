## Worker-side and firm-side channels

| Channel | Worker-side margin | Firm-side margin | Typical downstream outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| Reduced physical/mental capacity | Lower hours, exit, occupational downgrading | Screening, task reassignment, accommodation | Employment, hours, wages |
| Higher health risk | Search for safer/flexible jobs, retirement timing | Insurance provision, safety investment | Job choice, retention, amenities |
| Treatment access / insurance | Better recovery, job lock, mobility changes | Benefit design, job package differentiation | Turnover, matching, welfare |
| Health uncertainty | Precautionary labor supply or savings changes | Contracting around absenteeism/productivity risk | Attachment, scheduling, pay |
| Chronic condition burden | Reduced commuting tolerance, slower mobility | Flexible scheduling, redesign, monitoring | Mobility, job quality, welfare |
