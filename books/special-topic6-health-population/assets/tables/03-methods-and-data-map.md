## Data and empirical strategies

| Research object | Common design / method | Typical data | Main challenge |
|---|---|---|---|
| Child penalties / fertility timing | event study, sibling timing, IVF / fertility treatment quasi-experiments | registers, admin earnings, survey panels | timing endogeneity and anticipation |
| Family health shocks | event timing around severe illness/death | admin hospital/death registers + earnings records | separating income loss from care demand |
| Caregiving burdens | policy variation, linked family admin data, structural care models | admin panels, HRS/SHARE/ELSA, care surveys | hidden intensity and unmeasured informal care |
| Retirement timing | pension-age reforms, eligibility discontinuities, policy narratives | admin earnings, pension records, cross-country harmonized surveys | jointly moving pension and labor-demand conditions |
| Aging societies | cross-country comparative designs, institutional databases | ISS project, OECD, SHARE/ELSA/HRS, admin data | portability and institutional heterogeneity |
