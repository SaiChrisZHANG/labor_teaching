## Theory-to-empirical bridge

| Theory question | Applied interpretation | Observable labor margin | Typical empirical design |
|---|---|---|---|
| Who should see which jobs/workers? | Ranking / recommendation / invitation rule | Applications, interview rates, hires | A/B test or rule rollout |
| What should be disclosed? | Ratings, histories, wage info, employer messages | Search targeting, wage bids, match quality | Randomized disclosure / field experiment |
| How should pay be structured? | Posted wages, commissions, floors, dynamic pricing | Worker entry, hours, bids, fill rates | Wage-rule experiment or policy change |
| Who bears platform risk? | Earnings volatility, deactivation risk, dispute resolution | Continuation, hours, surplus, turnover | Platform policy change / admin panel |
| Does the platform improve or distort allocation? | Quality filtering vs steering vs power shift | Match quality, surplus, access | Structural estimation or reduced-form redesign |
