### Portability and relevance

| Common critique | A strong response |
|---|---|
| “This is just a niche matching market.” | Show the general labor-market mechanism: congestion, information, incentives, or allocation under constraints. |
| “The institution is too specific.” | Explain which design feature is portable and which institutional details are local. |
| “The paper is all mechanism design and no labor economics.” | Keep labor outcomes central: hiring, wages, effort, retention, training, worker welfare. |
| “The estimate is local and not welfare-relevant.” | State clearly who benefits, who loses, and what equilibrium responses are likely. |
| “The intervention improved a metric, but not necessarily welfare.” | Distinguish descriptive performance gains from worker, firm, or public welfare. |
