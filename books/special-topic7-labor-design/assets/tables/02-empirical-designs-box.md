:::{table} Common empirical designs for recruiting, congestion, timing, and unraveling
:label: tbl:empirical-designs-box

| Design family | Best use case | Representative labor application |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Regime comparison | before/after centralized clearing or timing reform | gastroenterology market with and without a match |
| Employer-side reduced form | vacancy yield, hiring speed, recruiting policy margins | establishment-level vacancies and recruiting intensity |
| Platform experiment | application caps, queue rules, disclosure changes | online labor market congestion or wage-disclosure experiment |
| Historical / descriptive institutional evidence | exploding offers, timing norms, market scope | clerkships, law-firm hiring, fellowship markets |
| Vacancy-level causal design | wage disclosure or posting design changes | applicant response to pay information |
| Structural / directed-search estimation | sorting and congestion under alternative rules | recruitment intensity or applicant-allocation models |
:::
