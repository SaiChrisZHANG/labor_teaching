### Research design template

| Element | Guiding question | Example in labor-market design |
|---|---|---|
| Mechanism | Which rule or institution changes behavior? | Deferred acceptance, exploding-offer ban, pay transparency rule |
| Labor object | What labor margin should move? | Match quality, vacancy yields, wages, effort, retention |
| Empirical leverage | Why is identification possible? | Pilot rollout, randomized visibility, rule threshold, administrative change |
| Counterfactual | Relative to what? | Decentralized market, old mechanism, non-treated units |
| Equilibrium response | What spillovers matter? | Congestion reallocation, employer substitution, waitlist dynamics |
| Welfare object | Whose welfare is at stake? | Workers, firms, trainees, patients, public agencies |
| External validity | Why should others care? | Common design problem, portable mechanism, labor-market relevance |
