:::{table} Core process failures in decentralized hiring
:label: tbl:core-process-failures

| Process stage | Design object | Typical friction | Labor outcome at stake |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Application choice | where/when workers apply | too many low-value applications, poor directed search | wasted search effort, congestion |
| Screening | interview shortlist, resume filter, recruiter bandwidth | too many applicants per slot | interview bottlenecks, low yield |
| Wage information | whether pay is disclosed or implied | opacity and misdirection | mis-sorting, search inefficiency |
| Offer timing | deadlines, exploding offers, early commitments | unraveling and risk-shifting | poor match quality, unfair timing |
| Acceptance/placement | decentralized vs centralized timing | panic acceptance or scope reduction | welfare loss, reduced market thickness |
:::
