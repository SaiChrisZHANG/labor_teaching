### Theory-to-empirical bridge

| Theoretical question | Observable labor-market object | Typical data | Applied research question |
|---|---|---|---|
| Is the market unstable? | Unfilled positions, rematching pressure, post-match renegotiation | Match outcomes, vacancy records, timing archives | Did a centralized match reduce blocking-pair type failures? |
| Is congestion causing unraveling? | Exploding offers, early acceptances, delayed information | Offer timing, acceptance timing, institutional rule changes | Do timing rules move offers later and improve match quality? |
| Do priorities or quotas alter welfare? | Geographic placement, underserved-area staffing, access by subgroup | Match rosters, location assignments, subgroup data | Who gains and loses when priority rules change? |
| Does centralized design change bargaining power? | Salaries, outside options, staffing shortages | Wage data, program characteristics, panel matches | Does the design alter rent division or only allocation? |
| Can design be improved counterfactually? | Simulated assignments under alternative rules | Rankings or inferred preferences, match data | Which policy reform improves staffing, fairness, or welfare? |
