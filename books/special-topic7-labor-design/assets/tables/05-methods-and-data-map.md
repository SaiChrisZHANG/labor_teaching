## Methods and data

| Method/design | When useful | Data often used | What it identifies |
|---|---|---|---|
| Before/after redesign with rich institutional detail | A centralized rule is replaced | Administrative match data, rankings, offers | Changes in allocations, but vulnerable without a control |
| Randomized or quasi-randomized market-level design | The mechanism is assigned across markets | Market-by-market assignment and outcomes | Causal effect of the mechanism [@davisGreenbergJones2026] |
| RDD around ranking / score cutoffs | Public hiring or training admission thresholds | Application scores, assignments, later outcomes | Local effect of assignment or entry |
| Recruitment-stage randomization | Contract terms vary before entry | Applicant pool + post-entry performance | Selection vs effort margins [@leaverOzierSerneelsSabarwal2021] |
| Matched worker–job internal market data | Internal reassignment or promotion systems | Worker preferences, job bids, manager rankings | Internal allocation, ladder mobility, mismatch |
| Preference + priority elicitation | Hidden objectives / tradeoffs matter | Surveys + admin data | Whether the mechanism aligns with actual objectives |
