### Frontier project opportunities

| Topic | A promising question | Useful data/design |
|---|---|---|
| Within-vacancy design | How do employers screen applicants under interview bottlenecks? | Application-level admin data, randomized ranking/visibility |
| Internal assignment | How do assignment rules affect later promotions and retention? | Personnel panels, policy pilots, structured assignment reform |
| Platform wage rules | Do pricing or transparency rules change match quality or worker risk? | Platform A/B tests, staggered rule changes, panel outcomes |
| Public staffing | How do rules balance fairness and hard-to-staff locations? | Administrative assignment data, priority reforms, staffing outcomes |
| Professional entry | How do market design changes affect career starts and long-run mobility? | Match data linked to later earnings / workplace outcomes |
| Welfare and equilibrium | When do design improvements merely shift rents rather than improve allocation? | Structural counterfactuals + reduced-form incidence evidence |
