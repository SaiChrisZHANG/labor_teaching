## Recommended lab logic
- **Primary reproduce anchor:** Lazear (2000)
- **Diagnose:** incentive effect vs sorting effect; measurable output vs hidden distortions
- **Transfer:** apply the same logic to a subjective-evaluation or commitment setting
- **Challenge extension anchor:** de Janvry et al. (2023) or Kaur, Kremer, and Mullainathan (2015)
