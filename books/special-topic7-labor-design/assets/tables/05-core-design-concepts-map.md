## Core concepts

| Object | Labor question | Design margin |
|---|---|---|
| Centralized clearing | Who gets which job/training slot? | Stability, priorities, truthful revelation |
| Public staffing objective | Can underserved units or places be staffed? | Priority rules, reserved slots, service obligations |
| Wage rigidity / compressed pay | How much of allocation must be done by nonprice rules? | Matching, priorities, contracts, queues |
| Internal labor market | How are workers reassigned after entry? | Job ladders, bidding, promotion, managerial discretion |
| Outside options | Who enters or exits the market? | Public/private compensation, mission value, security |
| Training pipeline | How do assignment rules affect later careers? | Initial placement, probation, rotation, retention |
