### Where the field is and where it is going

| Area | What the literature has done well | Open frontier |
|---|---|---|
| Centralized matches | Stable matching, preference estimation, policy counterfactuals | Richer heterogeneity, long-run outcomes, multi-market interaction |
| Recruiting design | Timing, exploding offers, congestion, applicant information | Within-vacancy design, interview bottlenecks, AI-assisted recruiting |
| Contracts and incentives | Performance pay, screening, moral hazard | Dynamic contracts, subjective evaluation, algorithmic supervision |
| Platforms | Visibility, reputation, wage posting, flexibility | Ranking algorithms, opaque pricing, multi-sided welfare |
| Public/professional systems | Teacher, residency, army, public assignment systems | Career ladders, mobility across systems, political/public objectives |
| Methods | Field experiments, audits, admin data, structural models | Better welfare measurement, equilibrium diagnosis, portability |
