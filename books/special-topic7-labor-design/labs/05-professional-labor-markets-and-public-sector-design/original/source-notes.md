# Source Notes

The teaching path is inspired by the Week 5 reading architecture:

- deferred acceptance and priority design in Army officer assignment;
- matching with contracts in military branch assignment;
- teacher and public-service placement;
- public-sector recruitment contracts;
- internal ladders, training, promotion, and on-the-job search;
- public/private outside options and exit.

No confidential personnel records, official assignment files, military branch preferences, school placement systems, civil-service microdata, or official replication packages are included.
