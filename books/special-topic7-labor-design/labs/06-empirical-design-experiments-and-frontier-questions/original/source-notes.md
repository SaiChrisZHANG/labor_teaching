# Source Notes

The lab uses deterministic synthetic data because full replication access is uncertain and because the Week 6 learning goal is research architecture rather than exact replication.

The synthetic path preserves the teaching objects needed for the capstone:

- mechanism-first assignment design;
- counterfactual comparison across rules;
- worker preference and program priority margins;
- shortage-site staffing and welfare components;
- equilibrium and portability diagnostics.
