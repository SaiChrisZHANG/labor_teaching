### Firm-response taxonomy

| Response type | Canonical firm problem | Worker-side wedge | Observable labor-market manifestation | Representative papers |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Exploit | Design menus/defaults that profit from inertia or inattention | Limited attention, switching costs, choice complexity | sticky plan participation, low demand elasticity, fee/benefit incidence | [@bernheimFradkinPopov2015]; [@duarteHastings2012]; [@hastingsHortacsuSyverson2017] |
| Accommodate | Simplify choice or redesign information environment to reduce mistakes | confusion, information frictions, misperception | simpler menus, disclosure redesign, reduced mistakes, changed take-up | [@handelKolstad2015] |
| Insure | Use defaults or employer intermediation to protect workers from poor decisions | inertia, low salience, limited sophistication | welfare-improving automatic enrollment / plan design | [@bernheimFradkinPopov2015]; [@handelKolstad2015] |
| Screen / sort / segment | Offer contracts/messages that separate behavioral types or direct applications | distorted beliefs, heterogeneous sophistication, response heterogeneity | application sorting, persistent low-wage matches, segmented demand | [@jagerRothRoussilleSchoefer2022]; [@hortonKuchlerStanton2021] |
