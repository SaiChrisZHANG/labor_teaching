### Frontier questions and empirical gaps

| Frontier question | Why existing evidence is incomplete | Promising data / design direction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| When do employer defaults improve welfare versus exploit inertia? | observed participation effects do not pin down optimality under endogenous menus | linked payroll-plan data, menu redesign shocks, welfare decomposition |
| How much do distorted outside-option beliefs contribute to labor-market segmentation? | worker beliefs are measured better than equilibrium incidence | worker survey + admin match data, information shocks, bargaining/search outcomes |
| Which market structures discipline behavioral exploitation? | evidence is scattered across contexts | comparative studies of competition, disclosure, and market design |
| How far do employer messages reshape application sorting and wage bids? | signaling evidence is still thin in labor settings | randomized employer communication, platform data, equilibrium application flows |
| Can external-validity failures in nudges be explained by endogenous firm response? | most nudge evidence is locally identified and partial-equilibrium | policy rollouts with firm adaptation margins, market-wide disclosure changes |
