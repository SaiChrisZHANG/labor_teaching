:::{table} Job-search margins and where behavior enters
:label: tbl:job-search-margins

| Margin | Standard benchmark | Behavioral wedge | Representative paper |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Search intensity | chosen from correct payoff beliefs | optimism, procrastination, misperceived returns | [@dellaVignaPaserman2005] |
| Reservation strategy | based on correct offer distribution | biased beliefs and weak updating | [@muellerSpinnewijnTopa2021] |
| Application targeting | based on true fit and posted information | misperceived competitiveness or self-fit | [@belotKircherMuller2022] |
| Signaling / certification | worker quality taken as given or hidden | workers learn about themselves and strategically reveal | [@carranzaGarlickOrkinRankin2022] |
| Search strategy formation | agents know effective search channels | advice and information alter strategy quality | [@altmannFalkJaegerZimmermann2018] |
| Application-cost response | costs shift search smoothly | small frictions sharply distort who applies | [@abebeCariaOrtizOspina2021] |
