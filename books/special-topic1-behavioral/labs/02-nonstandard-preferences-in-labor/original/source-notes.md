# Source Notes

The synthetic reproduction file is inspired by [@kaurKremerMullainathan2010] and [@kaurKremerMullainathan2015]. It is not an official replication file.

Teaching objects:

- contract choice: standard or commitment;
- daily workplace output;
- target completion;
- payday distance;
- commitment take-up.

The design question is whether observed workplace effort identifies only standard incentives or also demand for commitment against future self-control problems.
