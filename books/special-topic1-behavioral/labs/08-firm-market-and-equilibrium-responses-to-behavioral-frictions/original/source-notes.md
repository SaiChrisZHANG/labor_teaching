# Source Notes

The local data are synthetic and are generated by `src/build_week8_synthetic_data.py`. They represent a compact employer-sponsored retirement-default environment with variation in default contribution rates, active choice, worker sophistication, liquidity needs, passive choice, and welfare-distance diagnostics.

The design is inspired by the teaching logic of [@bernheimFradkinPopov2015]. It is not an official replication package and does not use confidential employer records.
