# Source Notes

The synthetic reproduction file is inspired by [@royerStehrSydnor2015]. It is not an official replication file.

Teaching objects:

- treatment arm: control, incentive, commitment, and incentive plus commitment;
- baseline participation;
- program-period participation;
- post-program persistence;
- commitment take-up.

The design question is whether a labor-facing wellness intervention identifies only a short-run incentive response or also demand for commitment and durable habit formation.
