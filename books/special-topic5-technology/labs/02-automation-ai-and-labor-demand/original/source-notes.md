# Source Notes

The synthetic lab follows the Week 2 chapter logic:

1. define industry robot intensity;
2. aggregate industry exposure to commuting zones;
3. compare employment and wage incidence;
4. diagnose why quantities and wages can move differently;
5. transfer the logic to firm AI adoption and vacancy AI demand.

The primary pedagogical anchor is Acemoglu and Restrepo's local robot exposure design [@acemogluRestrepo2020Robots]. The challenge comparison is Aghion, Bergeaud, Boppart, Klenow, and Li's firm-level AI adoption evidence [@aghionBergeaudBoppartKlenowLi2025differentUsesAI]. The lab does not use official data from either paper.
