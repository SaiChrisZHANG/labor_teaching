# Transfer Data Notes

The transfer data are generated inside `src/automation_ai_labor_demand_lab.py`.

Synthetic firms differ in workforce task exposure, realized AI adoption, vacancy AI demand, and output-demand growth. These fields are chosen to make the labor-demand contrast visible:

- task exposure from workforce composition;
- realized AI use from firm adoption intensity;
- desired labor demand from vacancy AI intensity;
- output-demand offsets from firm growth.
