# Transfer Data Notes

The transfer outputs are synthetic. A real project would need data that match the chosen unit:

- worker use: usage logs, survey data, personnel records, productivity measures, wages, promotion, and mobility;
- firm adoption: adoption surveys, vendor records, vacancies, training records, employment, wages, and organization measures;
- platform governance: rule changes, ratings, scheduling, pay, disputes, deactivations, and worker voice;
- compute infrastructure: data-center locations, grid capacity, electricity prices, construction employment, technical operations jobs, and local service demand;
- global service trade: task-level service flows, outsourcing exposure, AI adoption, sectoral employment, wages, formality, and job quality.
