# Source Notes

The synthetic path is inspired by:

- firm AI-investment and workforce-composition evidence in Babina, Fedyk, He, and Hodson;
- firm-level AI adoption and use-case heterogeneity in Aghion et al.;
- algorithm-aversion and delegation designs in Dargnies, Hakimov, and Kubler;
- survey-experimental work on algorithmic and human decision-makers.

No proprietary resume data, job-posting microdata, employer-employee records, vendor telemetry, or official replication files are included.
