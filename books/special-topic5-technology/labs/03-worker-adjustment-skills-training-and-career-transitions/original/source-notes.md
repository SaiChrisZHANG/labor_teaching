# Source Notes

The local script constructs deterministic synthetic workers, task shares, technology weights, firm adoption indicators, local adjustment capacity, and worker outcomes.

The script is designed to teach the logic of:

- worker-level task exposure;
- dynamic earnings and employment incidence;
- training, switching, and exit as distinct adjustment margins;
- transfer to local training or retirement responses;
- transfer to incumbent versus new-hire training under AI adoption.

No official paper estimates are reproduced.
