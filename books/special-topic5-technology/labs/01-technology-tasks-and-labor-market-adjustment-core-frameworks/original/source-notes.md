# Source Notes

The synthetic lab follows the Week 1 chapter logic:

1. define technology descriptors;
2. map descriptors to task categories;
3. aggregate task exposure to occupations;
4. compare measurement choices;
5. transfer the measurement problem to firms.

The primary pedagogical anchor is Webb's technology-to-task exposure logic [@webb2020]. The measurement contrast is Kogan, Papanikolaou, Schmidt, and Seegmiller's worker-level technology exposure logic [@koganPapanikolaouSchmidtSeegmiller2023]. The lab does not use official data from either paper.
