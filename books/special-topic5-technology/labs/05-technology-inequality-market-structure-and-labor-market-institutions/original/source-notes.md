# Source Notes

The synthetic path is inspired by:

- superstar-firm, labor-share, and concentration evidence in Autor, Dorn, Katz, Patterson, and Van Reenen;
- structural change within versus across firms in Ding, Fort, Michaels, Morrow, and Schott;
- establishment-level technology sophistication evidence in Cirera, Comin, Cruz, Lee, and Martins;
- frontier questions on AI, outsourcing, electricity demand, and data-center geography.

No confidential firm microdata, linked employer-worker records, official Economic Census files, establishment survey microdata, data-center vendor files, or official replication files are included.
