## Research Design Template

| Design element | Questions to answer |
|---|---|
| Labor object | What is the labor-market outcome: employment, wages, hours, vacancies, training, promotion, mobility, labor share, bargaining power, or welfare? |
| Technology margin | Is the core object invention, adoption, worker use, task exposure, vacancy demand, organizational implementation, or market diffusion? |
| Unit of analysis | Worker, occupation, firm, establishment, commuting zone, local labor market, or country? |
| Comparison / counterfactual | What makes the variation informative: exposed vs less exposed, before vs after, adopter vs non-adopter, treated vs untreated geography, experimental contrast? |
| Adjustment channel | Through labor demand, worker reskilling, firm reorganization, market power, or institutional response? |
| Equilibrium interpretation | Is the object partial-equilibrium incidence or closer to equilibrium adjustment? |
| Welfare object | Why are wages or employment alone not enough? What happens to risk, bargaining power, job quality, or access? |
| Portability | What is specific to the setting and what should travel more broadly? |
