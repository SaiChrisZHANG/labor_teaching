:::{table} From empirical setting to measurement choice
:label: tbl:setting-measurement-map
| Empirical setting | Good first-pass technology measure | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Occupation-level exposure to AI or automation | Patent-text or task-based exposure scores | Links capabilities to task content |
| Industry or region exposed to embodied automation | Robot adoption / installation data | Captures physical automation directly |
| Firm reorganization after digital adoption | Firm-level software / AI / IT adoption measures | Closer to actual implementation |
| Hiring and skill demand response | Job-posting text and vacancy data | Observes employer requests in real time |
| Worker displacement and long-run earnings | Worker-level exposure from patents/tasks matched to occupations | Maps exposure to worker careers |
| Innovation without immediate adoption | Patent data and invention timing | Useful when the question is about knowledge creation |
:::
