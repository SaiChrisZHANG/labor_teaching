# Methods and Data Map

| Design family | What it is good for | Common data |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Market-share decomposition | separating within-firm from between-firm labor-share changes | Economic Census, Compustat, Orbis, national accounts |
| Concentration / labor-share accounting | relating technology-driven reallocation to concentration and wages | Economic Census, firm panels, payroll data |
| Shift-share / exposure designs | local or industry exposure to automation, trade, or digital tech | commuting-zone data, industry employment, occupation-task measures |
| Long-run historical comparison | persistence, reversal, and complementarity across technology waves | historical firm/plant records, industrial censuses, patents |
| Structural-change decomposition | reallocation across agriculture, manufacturing, and services | sectoral employment panels, establishment microdata |
| Cross-country establishment surveys | technology sophistication and adoption in development contexts | World Bank firm surveys, FAT/technology surveys |
| Frontier infrastructure / energy data | AI-related electricity, data-center location, and local labor incidence | IEA, grid/interconnection data, utility data, spatial infrastructure data |
