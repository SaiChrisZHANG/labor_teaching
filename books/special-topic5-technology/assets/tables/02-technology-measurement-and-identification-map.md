## Technology measurement and identification map

| Technology object | What it actually measures | Typical unit | Strength | Main limitation | Representative papers |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Industrial robots | embodied automation capital | industry, commuting zone, firm | clear substitution story | misses software/AI reorganization | [@acemogluRestrepo2020Robots]; [@graetzMichaels2018Robots] |
| Task exposure indices | susceptibility of tasks to substitution/augmentation | occupation, industry | connects well to theory | exposure is not adoption | [@autorLevyMurnane2003SkillContent] |
| Patent/text-based technology exposure | direction/content of innovation | worker, occupation, firm | captures innovation frontier | far from realized implementation | [@koganPapanikolaouSchmidtSeegmiller2023] |
| Firm AI adoption | realized organizational adoption | firm, establishment | close to treatment | selection into adoption is severe | [@aghionBergeaudBoppartKlenowLi2025differentUsesAI]; [@hampoleMontesOberfield2025] |
| Worker-level AI use | actual usage and productivity response | worker/team | reveals within-job augmentation | often narrow setting / short horizon | [@brynjolfssonLiRaymond2025GenerativeAI] |
| Job postings / vacancy text | desired task mix and skill demand | firm, occupation, local market | forward-looking demand signal | vacancies are not actual employment | [@huiReshefZhou2025shortTermGenAI] |
