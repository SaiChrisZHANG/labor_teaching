## Where the field is / Where it is going

| Area | What is already strong | What remains open |
|---|---|---|
| Task-based frameworks | Strong theory and convincing evidence on routine task displacement and automation | Better mapping from new AI tools to actual workplace tasks |
| Labor demand incidence | Mature local-exposure and robot-incidence literatures | Reconciling local-exposure designs with firm-adoption and worker-use evidence |
| Worker adjustment | Strong work on reallocation, retraining, retirement, and career effects | Measuring behavioral and informational frictions in adaptation |
| Firm adoption | Fast-growing evidence on AI investment, adoption, and implementation | Better identification of adoption vs usage vs organizational redesign |
| Market structure and rents | Strong work on superstar firms, labor share, and inequality | How technology changes labor-market power and worker voice in real time |
| Global and infrastructure questions | Emerging work on Global South adoption, outsourcing, and compute demand | Electricity, data-center geography, environmental incidence, and global service chains |
