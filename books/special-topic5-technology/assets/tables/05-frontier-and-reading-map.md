## Frontier questions
1. When do successive technology waves cancel earlier labor-displacing effects, and when do they reinforce them?
2. Does AI deepen superstar-firm dynamics or create a distinct infrastructure bottleneck through energy and compute?
3. When workers leave manufacturing, what predicts whether they move into good service jobs versus low-quality services?
4. How should labor economists think about outsourced digital work and data-labor supply in the Global South?
5. Which institutions prevent productivity gains from translating into worker welfare gains?
