### Firm adoption and organization map

| Object | Labor question | Typical evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Adoption decision | Which firms adopt and why? | management surveys, firm adoption surveys, patents, software/vendor data |
| Job redesign | Which tasks/jobs are rebundled? | job postings, worker resumes, occupational task data |
| Hiring response | Do firms hire different workers after adoption? | vacancy text, employer–employee data, matched resumes |
| Training/internal mobility | Do firms retrain or replace incumbents? | HR records, internal personnel data, linked worker panels |
| Supervision/monitoring | Does technology change effort monitoring or contracting? | field experiments, implementation pilots, digital trace data |
| Delegation/authority | Who decides: worker, manager, or algorithm? | experiments, workflow data, supervisory records |
