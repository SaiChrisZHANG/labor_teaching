## Portability and Relevance Map

| Issue | Common weak framing | Better labor-economics framing |
|---|---|---|
| Technology focus | “I study AI because AI is important.” | “I study how a measurable technology margin changes a labor outcome through a specific adjustment channel.” |
| Setting-specific evidence | “This firm/sector/country is interesting.” | “This setting reveals a general labor mechanism that is hard to observe elsewhere.” |
| Exposure vs adoption | “Exposure predicts outcomes.” | “Exposure is informative because it approximates a specific labor-demand or organizational margin.” |
| Welfare | “Employment went up/down.” | “The key object is wages, mobility, bargaining power, job quality, risk, or worker welfare.” |
| External validity | “This should generalize.” | “Here is what should transport, what should not, and why.” |
