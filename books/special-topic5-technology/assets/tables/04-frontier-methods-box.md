### Frontier methods box

| Design | What it identifies well | Main limitation |
|---|---|---|
| Management survey + firm outcomes | managerial expectations and adoption beliefs | difficult to separate beliefs from firm quality |
| Worker survey linked to usage/outcomes | worker trust, resistance, perceived fairness | attitudes may be endogenous to rollout quality |
| Randomized pilot / staggered rollout | causal effect of tool introduction on workers/teams | may miss long-run organizational redesign |
| Lab / online delegation experiment | algorithm aversion, trust, fairness perceptions | external validity to real workplaces |
| Conjoint / vignette experiment | acceptance of AI hiring/evaluation rules | scenario preferences may not match actual adoption |
| Matched employer–employee event study | worker incidence around adoption timing | identifying adoption itself remains hard |
| Job-posting text linked to adoption | hiring/task redesign | says more about demand than realized usage |
