### Firm response and welfare map

| Policy area | Worker-side mechanism | Firm-side adjustment | Welfare objects to track |
|---|---|---|---|
| Childcare | time constraint relief, bargaining relief | schedule redesign, occupation-specific accommodation | worker earnings, household income, child development, firm productivity |
| Leave | continuity and insurance around birth | staffing substitution, promotion timing, retention policy | attachment, long-run careers, child/family welfare, firm turnover costs |
| Taxes | second-earner incentive / disincentive | compensation mix, contract choice, labor demand by household type | labor supply efficiency, distribution within household, fiscal incidence |
| Quotas | access to authority and networks | pipeline building, hiring rules, mentoring, internal reassignment | representation, spillovers, rent access, efficiency |
| Transparency / equal pay | bargaining and information frictions | pay compression, bonus redesign, segregation, promotion rule changes | wage equality, career growth, firm rents, distortion costs |
| Protection / enforcement | safety, reporting credibility, anti-retaliation | compliance investment, sorting, formal documentation | retention, risk exposure, dignity, outside options |
