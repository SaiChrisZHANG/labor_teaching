| Data family | Main labor objects seen well | Typical gender applications | What it still misses |
|---|---|---|---|
| Matched employer--employee data | Firm premiums, promotions, supervisor chains, retention, within- vs between-firm gaps | Firm-side pay setting, promotion ladders, segregation across firms | Hidden harms, unpaid work, beliefs, unobserved nonemployment |
| Administrative panels | Earnings dynamics, childbirth timing, policy incidence, job mobility | Child penalties, leave reforms, timing of exits and reentry | Informal work, unmeasured care burdens, workplace climate |
| Audits and field experiments | Hiring, callback, evaluation, screening, informational frictions | Hiring discrimination, screening design, recruitment messages | Long-run equilibrium effects, internal careers |
| Surveys and expectations data | Aspirations, beliefs, bargaining expectations, anticipated discrimination | Education choices, major choice, willingness to negotiate, perceived barriers | Behavioral truthfulness, sample size, panel persistence |
| Time-use data | Unpaid labor, care time, scheduling, invisible work | Care burdens, home production, time reallocation after children or policy | Firm-side treatment, wages if not linked |
| Job postings and text-as-data | Employer demand, job design, stated constraints, language | Gendered job requests, flexibility, culture signals, ad language | Realized treatment after hiring |
| Platform / transaction data | High-frequency labor supply, task choice, hours, experience, pricing | Gig earnings gaps, flexibility tradeoffs, timing responses | Broader institutions, nonplatform careers |
| Gender identity / climate measures | Identity categories, workplace culture, climate, belonging | LGBTQ+ labor outcomes, nonbinary discrimination, climate and retention | Small cells, privacy, comparability over time |
