:::{table} Portability, relevance, and critique
:label: tbl:gender-portability-relevance

| Question | Strong answer | Common critique to avoid |
|---|---|---|
| Why this setting? | The setting isolates a labor mechanism unusually cleanly | “This country is interesting because it is unusual” |
| What travels? | The mechanism or margin is likely portable even if levels are not | Claiming universal relevance from a deeply local institutional design |
| What does not travel? | Legal, cultural, or sectoral specifics that mediate the effect | Ignoring institutional context |
| Why should non-experts care? | The result informs a broader labor-market question | Assuming relevance is obvious because gender inequality matters |
| What is the labor contribution? | The study teaches something about work, wages, firms, households, institutions, mobility, or welfare | Framing the paper as only a social-issue description |

:::
