| Empirical setting | Common observed margin | Common econometric tools | Typical gender question |
|---|---|---|---|
| Childbirth or family shock with long panel | Earnings / hours / employment event time | Event studies, dynamic treatment effects, decomposition by outcome | Where does the child penalty come from? |
| Matched worker--firm panel | Within- vs between-firm wage gap | Worker and firm fixed effects, AKM-style decompositions, mover designs | How much of the gap is firm sorting vs firm treatment? |
| Resume / hiring audit | Callback / interview / offer | Randomized signals, treatment-effect heterogeneity, audit comparisons | Does the screening stage treat applicants differently? |
| Job-board reform or posting ban | Applications, callbacks, match composition | Difference-in-differences, event studies, ad-level quasi-experiments | How do explicit constraints in demand shape sorting? |
| Time-use diary linked to labor outcomes | Care time, market hours, multitasking | Time-budget decompositions, panel regressions, pre/post designs | How do home-production burdens map into market work? |
| Platform / transaction panel | Hours, acceptance, task choice, effective earnings | Within-worker comparisons, decomposition of earnings sources, dynamic learning designs | Which behavioral or scheduling margins generate gaps? |
| Workplace climate / harassment linked to admin outcomes | Complaints, turnover, earnings, workplace exit | Linked survey-admin analysis, hazard models, selection corrections | How do hidden harms affect welfare and retention? |
| Survey or expectation module | Beliefs, bargaining expectations, anticipated discrimination | Survey experiments, vignette randomization, belief updates | How do expectations shape pre-market and workplace choices? |
