### Policy as identification device

| Policy shock | Underlying gender question identified | Common empirical designs | Common pitfalls |
|---|---|---|---|
| Childcare expansion | How large are time constraints and care complementarities? | RCTs, staggered rollouts, DID, event studies | conflating supply effects with equilibrium demand shifts |
| Paid leave reform | Are child penalties driven by attachment or dynamic career penalties? | event studies at birth, RD in duration/eligibility, admin panels | treating short-run attachment as long-run convergence |
| Tax reform / separate taxation | How elastic is secondary-earner labor supply? | tax kinks, bunching, structural calibration, cross-country reforms | mismeasuring household incidence |
| Board or promotion quotas | Where do leadership bottlenecks actually sit? | reform exposure by firm type / eligibility, panel admin data | mistaking top-node change for broad pipeline change |
| Transparency laws | Are pay gaps driven by bargaining, bonuses, or promotion structures? | threshold DID, event studies, matched employer-employee panels | ignoring composition or public-scrutiny channels |
| Equal-pay rules | How do firms adjust when within-firm price discrimination is constrained? | regulatory reform, firm-worker panels, wage-structure decomposition | focusing on average gaps while missing segregation responses |
| Legal protections / reporting reform | How much do safety and credibility shape labor supply and retention? | policy rollout, administrative reporting data, matched panels | underreporting and endogenous reporting behavior |
