### Policy-to-margin incidence map

| Policy domain | Primary labor margins | Typical incidence questions | Typical firm response questions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Childcare and care support | participation, hours, self-employment, job quality | mothers vs fathers vs children vs households | scheduling, hiring, promotion timing, flexible arrangements |
| Parental leave | attachment, tenure, return-to-work, earnings trajectories | mothers vs fathers vs coworkers vs future careers | leave coverage, replacement hiring, promotion delay, pipeline effects |
| Taxation and transfers | participation, hours, secondary-earner entry, household specialization | second earners vs primary earners vs household bargaining | compensation design, formalization, contract form |
| Quotas and representation rules | promotion, leadership access, top-level authority, spillovers | targeted women vs near-miss candidates vs younger cohorts | pipeline management, search intensity, internal reallocation |
| Transparency and equal-pay rules | wages, bonuses, promotions, negotiation, within-firm dispersion | lower-paid women vs higher-paid men vs all employees | bonus compression, promotion criteria, job segregation |
| Legal protections and anti-discrimination rules | hiring, retention, reporting, mobility, safety, welfare | protected workers vs non-protected workers vs firms | compliance, documentation, screening, workplace redesign |
