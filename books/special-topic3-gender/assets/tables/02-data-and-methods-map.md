### Table. Data and methods commonly used in this literature

| Research question | Common data | Typical econometric methods | Representative papers |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| How large are child penalties? | Administrative earnings panels | Event study, panel FE | [@klevenLandaisPoschSchmidtSogaard2019] |
| What is the causal effect of fertility? | IVF/infertility data, birth records, long panels | IV, reduced form | [@lundborgPlugRasmussen2017], [@agueroMarks2008] |
| How does timing of childbirth matter? | Panel earnings and fertility histories | Reduced form, event study | [@buckles2008] |
| How do childcare constraints affect labor supply? | Policy/experimental program data | RCT, DID, policy evaluation | [@bjorvatnFerrisJayachandran2025] |
| How do bargaining and control over resources matter? | Household expenditure and labor-supply panels | Collective-model estimation, reduced form, structural methods | [@chiappori1992], [@mazzocco2014] |
| How do norms shape household choices? | Household surveys, matched couples, norm surveys | Reduced form, bunching/discontinuity-style evidence, survey experiment | [@bertrandKamenicaPan2015], [@bursztynGonzalezYanagizawaDrott2020] |
