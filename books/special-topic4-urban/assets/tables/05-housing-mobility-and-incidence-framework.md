## Housing, mobility, and incidence framework

| Object | Labor question | Typical observable | Interpretation risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| Wages | Do workers share in local productivity gains? | Earnings, hourly wages | Ignores rents and commuting costs |
| Rents / housing prices | Who captures place-based gains? | Rent indices, house prices | Not all workers are homeowners/renters equally |
| Migration | Who can move to opportunity or away from decline? | Inflows/outflows, address changes | Selection into moving obscures incidence |
| Commuting | Can workers access gains without moving? | Commute flows, travel times | Misses within-place composition effects |
| Employment / participation | Who remains attached after shocks? | Employment, LFPR | Place recovery can hide worker scarring |
| Firm entry / exit | Does labor demand create new job ladders locally? | Establishment births/deaths | Hard to separate demand from real-estate channel |
