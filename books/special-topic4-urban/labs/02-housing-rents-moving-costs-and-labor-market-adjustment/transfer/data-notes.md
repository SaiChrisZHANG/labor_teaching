# Transfer Data Notes

The transfer file is `transfer/data/local_demand_shocks_synthetic.csv`.

Each row is a synthetic place-by-group observation. The file is designed to illustrate local-demand incidence logic:

- demand shocks can raise wages and employment;
- rent growth can offset nominal gains for renters;
- ownership can convert price increases into capital gains;
- commuting openness can let nonresidents access job growth;
- moving-cost constraints can exclude would-be migrants from productive places.

These data are teaching artifacts only. They are not official replication data for Notowidigdo [@notowidigdo2020LocalDemandShocks].
